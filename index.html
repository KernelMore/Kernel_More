Our Story

Kernel & More was started by a passionate 20-year-old entrepreneur with a simple goal — to create delicious instant popcorn using premium American corn and high quality oil.

Every packet is made with carefully selected ingredients to deliver rich flavor, great crunch and consistent quality. Our goal is simple: bring movie-theatre style popcorn experience to every home.
